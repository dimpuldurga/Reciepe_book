Ingredients:
  • 1/4 cup rice (washed)
    		•
  • 5 cups milk
    		•
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust according to taste)
    		•
  • 4-5 green cardamom pods (powdered)
    		•
  • Handful of sliced almonds, cashews, and pistachios
    		•
  • A few strands of saffron (optional)
    		•
Method:
Step 1-
In a heavy-bottomed pan, bring 5 cups of milk to a boil.Once the milk is boiling, drain all the water from 1/4 cup of washed rice and add it to the hot milk. Mix well

Step 3-
Cook the rice on low heat without covering the pan until the rice is 50% done or half-cooked .

Step 4-
Add sugar according to your taste and mix it well with the milk . Continue to cook the rice on low to medium-low heat, stirring occasionally, until the rice is almost cooked .
Step 5-
Add 1 teaspoon of powdered green cardamom, sliced almonds, cashews, and pistachios to the kheer. Mix well . If desired, you can infuse the milk with saffron by dry roasting it in a saucepan for 30 seconds, adding 1 tablespoon of milk, and cooking it for 1 minute. Then add the saffron-milk mixture to the kheer .

Step 6-
Cook the kheer on a low flame until the rice is completely cooked and the kheer thickens . Once the rice grains are completely cooked and the kheer has thickened, switch off the heat ..

Step 7-
The kheer will thicken further as it cools down. You can scrape milk solids from the sides of the pan and add them to the kheer for added richness . Serve the kheer warm or chilled, garnished with more nuts .
