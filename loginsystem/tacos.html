Method:
Step 1-
Cook the white rice according to the package instructions..

Step 2-
In a separate pan, melt the butter over medium heat.

Step 3-
Add the lemon zest and sauté for a minute until fragrant. Pour in the lemon juice and stir well.

Step 4-
Add the cooked rice to the pan and mix until the rice is evenly coated with the lemon butter mixture. Season with salt to taste and stir again.

Step 5-
If desired, sprinkle fresh herbs like parsley or dill over the rice for added flavor. Serve the lemon rice as a side dish with your favorite meats or vegetables.
